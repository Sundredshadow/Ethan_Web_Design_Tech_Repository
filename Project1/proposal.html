I.High Concept

A small, intense, story driven, game focused on cyber security and hacking, and uses actual hacking principals, as the primary game mechanic.

II.Genre

Puzzle, Educational, Story Driven

III.Platform

Desktop (to start with maybe mobile)

IV.Story

The story will focus on actual hacking scenarios that are applicable to the average person. Will have the player act as the hacker, or act as sys admin or defender. It will also have additional mechanics that hopefully will push it beyond just a text based game, with actual gameplay, intertwined with the storyline and the pseudo-hacking/security.

V.Esthetics

Graphics: Pixel/2D; 8bit-32bit not sure; will mimic certain app like cmd.

VI.Gameplay

Integration of regular cyber security, with regular gameplay. Will have things like cmd, port scanning, etc as the cyber security mechanics, but will be done in a pseudo sense, so it's not as complicated and draining. The regular gameplay may include small hacking related minigames, or a small traditional 2D world to explore.
